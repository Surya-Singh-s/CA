Chartered Accountant (CA)

Chartered Accountants are responsible for financial accounting, auditing, and tax consulting. They ensure compliance with financial regulations and provide valuable insights into financial planning.

Company Secretary (CS)

Company Secretaries ensure that a company complies with legal and regulatory requirements. They manage corporate governance, advise the board of directors, and handle compliance matters.

Cost and Management Accountant (CMA)

CMA professionals focus on cost control and management strategies. They analyze financial data to assist in decision-making and help organizations optimize their operations and financial performance.
